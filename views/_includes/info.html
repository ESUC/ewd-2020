About

Engineering Welcome Day is an orientation event for roughly 700 incoming engineering freshmen and transfer students, hosted by the Engineering Society at UCLA (ESUC). Students will have the opportunity to learn about UCLA's School of Engineering, meet fellow engineering students, and hear from engineering faculty. Students will also have the opportunity to learn about the 40+ student organizations in attendance.

Engineering Welcome Day (EWD) will be hosted online on September 20th, 2020. The event will be split into two sessions to accommodate different time zones. Session 1 will be 9:00am - 12:00pm (PDT) and Session 2 will be 6:00pm - 9:00pm (PDT). Both sections are identical - plan to only attend one.

Note: This year, EWD is using a platform called Gatherly. Please note that Gatherly is only supported on a desktop/laptop and is not available on mobile devices. To test whether your computer can run Gatherly on a browser, please visit test.gatherly.io.
